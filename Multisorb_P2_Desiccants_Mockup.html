Which Desiccant Is Right for Your Product?

The right desiccant depends on your humidity target, packaging barrier, and compliance needs.

Desiccant materialBest atHolds moisture below 10% RH?Typical use
Silica gelGeneral-purpose control across a wide humidity rangePartiallyPharma, food, consumer goods, electronics
Molecular sieveAggressive removal at very low humidityYesDiagnostics, dry powder inhalers, high-barrier pharma
Clay (montmorillonite)Cost-effective protection at ambient temperaturesNoIndustrial, military (MIL-D-3716)

Frequently Asked Questions About Desiccants

What types of desiccants does Multisorb manufacture?
Silica gel, molecular sieve, clay, and activated-carbon-blend desiccants in packets, bags, canisters, adhesive labels, desiccant paper, injection-molded, and bulk formats, most of them customizable.
Which desiccant is best for pharmaceutical packaging?
For oral solid dose bottles, a dust-free desiccant canister (SorbiCap or Multiform CSF) is common; for very low humidity, molecular sieve is preferred. Multisorb supports DMF-referenced filings and ICH stability validation.
Are Multisorb desiccants FDA compliant and food safe?
Yes. Multisorb manufactures desiccants that meet FDA, USP, and EU requirements for food-contact and pharmaceutical applications, with documentation to support your qualification.
What is the difference between silica gel and molecular sieve?
Silica gel is a broad, general-purpose desiccant; molecular sieve removes moisture more aggressively and holds it at very low humidity. See molecular sieve vs. silica gel.
Desiccant canister or desiccant packet: which should I use?
Canisters offer dust-free, high-capacity protection ideal for pill and supplement bottles; packets are versatile and run easily on automated lines. Multisorb makes both and can advise based on your packaging.
Which desiccant is right for cannabis packaging?
For cannabis and hemp, moisture control that preserves potency and prevents mold is key, and products combining humidity control with oxygen scavenging are available for sensitive compounds. See cannabis solutions.
Can Multisorb customize desiccants and what are the minimums?
Yes. 90% of Multisorb products are customized to the application. Order minimums vary by product and format. Talk to an expert for specifics.
Do you provide documentation for regulated industries (DMF, COA)?
Yes. Multisorb supports pharmaceutical and nutraceutical qualification with the documentation those processes require. Contact us with your requirements.
